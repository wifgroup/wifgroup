WIFGroup Enters the Publishing World with Ishq-e-Adhura – A Story of Incomplete Love

We’re excited to announce a major milestone for WIFGroup — the launch of our first-ever publication, Ishq-e-Adhura by Harshil Chovatiya, now available as a Kindle eBook on Amazon India.

This emotionally rich Hindi romance novel explores the bittersweet journey of incomplete love, unspoken feelings, and the kind of heartbreak that stays with you forever.

💔 What is Ishq-e-Adhura About? A Deeply Emotional Love Story

Written by the talented Harshil Chovatiya, Ishq-e-Adhura is more than a romantic story — it’s a mirror of human emotions that readers will find painfully relatable. It takes you through:

  • The beauty and pain of unfinished love
  • Memories that refuse to fade
  • Emotions that words can barely contain

    • 🖋️ About the Author – Bhoomika badkas

    A passionate poet at heart, I find solace in weaving words that reflect the emotions within. Poetry is not just a hobby—it's my chosen language of expression. I love pouring my thoughts onto paper, believing it's the most powerful way to convey feelings that often go unspoken.
    For me, poetry is more than rhythm and rhyme—it's a mirror of the soul, a space where vulnerability meets strength. Every verse I write carries a piece of my journey, my dreams, and the quiet moments that shaped me. Through my words, I hope to connect with others, heal hearts, and remind readers that they are never alone in what they feel.

    🖋️ About the Author – Harshil Chovatiya

    Harshil Chovatiya is quickly becoming a well-loved name in contemporary Hindi literature. Known for his lyrical style and emotional depth, Harshil writes with a simplicity that resonates deeply with young readers across India.
    His stories are not just narratives — they are emotional experiences, and Ishq-e-Adhura is his most personal yet.

    🔍 Meaning Behind the Title – What Does “Ishq-e-Adhura” Mean?

    "Ishq-e-Adhura" translates to "Incomplete Love" — and that theme runs deep in every chapter. The book explores:

  • Love that never gets closure
  • The ache of separation
  • Memories that linger longer than the relationship itself

    • 📖 Story Summary – A Poetic Journey Through Love and Loss

    In this Hindi romance novel, two soulmates are bound by deep love but separated by life’s cruel timing. The story covers:

  • Their emotional connection
  • Their inner struggles and difficult choices
  • Heartbreaking turning points that change everything

    • It's a slow-burn, emotional journey that’s impossible to forget.

    ✍️ Writing Style – Poetry in Every Line

    CoHarshil’s writing feels like poetry in motion. His unique voice is:

  • 📝 Raw yet poetic
  • Relatable and heartfelt
  • ✨ Simple, but powerfully expressive

    • This is the kind of book you’ll underline, screenshot, and return to when emotions hit.

    ❤️ Why Readers Love Ishq-e-Adhura

    This Hindi love story has struck a deep emotional chord with readers. Here’s what makes it special:


  • Relatable: You’ll see your own memories in its pages
  • Emotional: It doesn’t hold back on the feels
  • Memorable: Full of quote-worthy lines

    • Accessible: Instantly available on Amazon Kindle And Google Play

  • Not all love stories need a happy ending
  • Vulnerability is powerful
  • Letting go doesn't always mean forgetting
