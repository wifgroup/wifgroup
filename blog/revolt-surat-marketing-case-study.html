Revolutionizing Revolt Surat: How WIF Group Transformed Their Digital Presence

In the ever-evolving landscape of digital marketing, standing out is no longer an option—it’s a necessity. Revolt Surat, a prominent player in the electric bike industry, faced a significant challenge in amplifying its digital reach and engagement. That’s where WIF Group stepped in to turn the game around.

The Challenges

Despite being a leading name in Surat’s electric bike market, Revolt Surat struggled with low online visibility. The brand had a minimal digital footprint, leading to reduced awareness and reach.

Another major hurdle was ineffective social media engagement. While the brand was present on Instagram and Facebook, their content failed to generate substantial engagement, leaving potential customers disconnected.

Additionally, the company faced a high cost per lead on ads. Running Meta ads resulted in a high cost per inquiry, making ad campaigns unsustainable in the long run.

Our Strategic Solutions

Revamping Content Strategy
We shifted from basic product showcases to engaging, story-driven content. By incorporating real customer experiences, behind-the-scenes insights, and interactive posts, we brought the brand closer to its audience.
Optimized Meta Ads for Maximum ROI
We completely overhauled their ad strategy by identifying and targeting the right audience segments. High-quality, visually appealing ad creatives were designed to capture attention instantly. Additionally, A/B testing was implemented to optimize ad performance, and the call-to-actions (CTAs) were refined for better conversions. As a result, the cost per inquiry dropped significantly, and engagement rates improved.
Leveraging Local Influencers & Community Building
To build brand trust, we collaborated with Surat-based influencers and micro-creators. Their authentic reviews and experiences generated organic reach and credibility for Revolt Surat, helping them connect with a wider audience

Before working with WIF Group, we struggled to create content that resonated with our customers. Now, our engagement has skyrocketed, and we feel more connected to our audience than ever!

Revolt Surat Team

The Results

Our tailored strategies led to remarkable improvements. Social media engagement saw a 100% increase, creating a more interactive and dynamic digital presence.

The cost per lead was reduced by 40%, making the ad campaigns far more sustainable and cost-effective. Additionally, Revolt Surat built a stronger brand identity and customer loyalty, translating into increased foot traffic and inquiries.
