about

A community built for people who are actively building

WIF Group started in Surat with a simple idea: the right introductions at the right time change outcomes. We built a space where founders, operators, mentors, and companies can find each other before urgent decisions become cold searches.

our story

Founded in 2023 in Surat, Gujarat

WIF Group was founded by Harshil Chovatiya with the belief that most small businesses fail not from lack of effort but from lack of access — to the right people, tools, and timing.

The community has grown to include founders at idea stage, established SMEs, digital and tech professionals, educators, and partner companies who want to build alongside the ecosystem rather than around it.

We are based in Surat with members across Gujarat and a growing partner network across India.

what we believe

Three things that guide how WIF works

01

Context before introduction

We onboard members around stage, goals, and current needs so every connection starts with relevant context — not a cold ask.

02

Useful over large

WIF is built for quality interaction, not scale. A smaller, active community where conversations lead somewhere is more valuable than a large, passive directory.

03

Learning through doing

Sessions, workshops, and feedback rooms are built around real problems members are facing today — not generic theory.

04

Long-term over transactional

We measure success by whether members grow — not by event attendance. WIF relationships are designed to compound over time.
