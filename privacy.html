1. Introduction

At WIF Group, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services, visit our website, or join the WIF Group community.

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other details necessary for providing our services or facilitating community membership. This includes information submitted via our contact form, enquiries, and community applications.
